# **App Name**: SpellSnap!

## Core Features:

- Homepage & Game Lobby: A central dashboard where users can start a new game, select game modes, difficulty levels, and access their profiles or administrative settings.
- Interactive Spelling Challenge: Core gameplay loop providing dynamic word displays, visual aids, and interactive spelling tasks (e.g., fill-in-the-blanks, scrambled letters) within a scrabble-like interface.
- Adaptive Difficulty & Themed Word Packs: Allows selection among Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced difficulty levels, featuring curated word lists organized by various educational themes for engaging practice.
- Teacher/Parent Word List Management: A dedicated interface for administrators to create, edit, delete, and assign custom spelling word lists, including options to associate words with images or definitions.
- AI-Powered Word List Assistant: An AI tool that assists teachers/parents in generating personalized spelling word lists based on input themes, grade levels, or specific learner needs, and can suggest definitions or example sentences.
- Learner Progress Dashboard: A visual and easy-to-understand dashboard displaying individual learner or class performance, tracking spelling accuracy, completed levels, and mastery of specific word lists.
- Gamified Reward System: Awards stars or points for correct answers and completed levels, allowing learners to unlock new themes, virtual rewards, or access higher difficulty tiers to foster motivation and progression.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: A vibrant, inviting orange-yellow (#F7A71C) for interactive elements and highlights, conveying energy and fun in learning.
- Background Color: A soft, desaturated off-white (#F9F3EC) providing a clean and friendly backdrop that is easy on the eyes for young learners.
- Accent Color: A warm, earthy red-orange (#B85122) used for secondary actions, important notifications, and to create visual contrast.
- Headline and Body Font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, clear, and highly legible characteristics, ensuring ease of reading for all text, especially for young learners.
- Use clear, friendly, and intuitive game-like icons and imagery that visually support the words and enhance the gamified learning experience for ESL students.
- The layout will feature a clean, interactive, and grid-based design for the spelling game, similar to scrabble, ensuring focused engagement with visual elements and word challenges. The overall design will prioritize clarity and ease of navigation for young users.
- Implement subtle and rewarding animations for correct answers, level completion, and navigation between game sections to provide positive feedback and enhance the interactive nature of the application.
